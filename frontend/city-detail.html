About Paris

Paris, the City of Light, captivates visitors with its iconic Eiffel Tower, world-class museums, and legendary cuisine. Stroll the cobblestone streets of Montmartre, cruise along the Seine, or sip café au lait at a terrace café. Every corner tells a story centuries in the making.

History

Founded by the Parisii tribe around 250 BC, Paris became the capital of France and one of Europe's leading centers of culture, commerce, fashion, gastronomy, and art since the 17th century.
